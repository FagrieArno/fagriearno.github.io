Research Interests

Molecular Plant Pathology

Molecular identification and characterization of plant pathogens using advanced techniques including qPCR, microsatellite markers, and genomic analysis.

Fusarium Research

Population analysis of Fusarium pseudograminearum affecting South African wheat and development of management strategies under conservation agriculture.

Disease Resistance

Investigation of defense-related genes and resistance mechanisms in crops, particularly maize resistance to Fusarium verticillioides.

Sustainable Agriculture

Development of integrated disease management strategies that support conservation agricultural practices and sustainable farming systems.
